FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

What is 01Talent?
+

01Talent is an international talent marketplace connecting companies with tech teams or engineering needs with AI-native engineers trained on the 01Edu platform, available internationally on offshoring missions.

What is an AI-native engineer?
+

An AI-native engineer is a developer trained from day one in an environment where AI is central, a default way of thinking, designing and building. They are selected on cognitive potential and problem-solving ability, regardless of academic background.

How does the hiring process work?
+

In three steps: you define your need (stack, volume, timeline), 01Talent identifies and qualifies matching profiles, then you meet pre-vetted engineers ready to be deployed on mission.

How long are 01Talent engineers trained?
+

Engineers complete 24 months of intensive, hands-on training on the 01Edu platform, through real-world projects in a peer-to-peer model, with no lectures, no teachers and no grades.

In which countries is 01Talent present?
+

01Talent operates offshoring centers in 5 countries: Greece (Athens), Morocco (Oujda), Kenya (Kisumu), Senegal (Dakar) and France (Rouen), plus a network of 10 partner academies across 4 continents.

What technologies do 01Talent engineers specialize in?
+

Engineers cover a broad range: AI/Data, Cybersecurity, Cloud, Mobile, Web, Blockchain, IoT/Robotics and custom software. Partner-specific technologies can also be integrated directly into the 01Edu curriculum.

How is 01Talent different from a traditional staffing agency?
+

01Talent owns the full chain, from talent identification and cognitive selection to training on 01Edu and deployment on mission. Every engineer in the 01Talent network was trained through the 01Edu platform, ensuring a consistent level of technical excellence, AI-native mindset, and life skills across all profiles.

Can the training be adapted to my company's tech stack?
+

Yes. Partner-specific technologies can be integrated directly into the 01Edu curriculum, so every engineer deployed arrives pre-trained on your exact environment.
