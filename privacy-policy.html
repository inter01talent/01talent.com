01 Who We Are

01Talent is an international talent agency connecting companies with AI-native engineers trained on the 01Edu platform. This Privacy Policy explains how 01Talent ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data when you visit www.01talent.com or interact with us.

01Talent acts as the data controller for personal data collected through this website. For data processed on behalf of our clients (for example, engineer candidate data shared during a staffing mission), 01Talent acts as a data processor under a separate data processing agreement.

Data Controller: 01Talent

Email: privacy@01talent.com

Address: 9 Rua da Mouraria, 9000-047 Funchal, Portugal

02 Data We Collect

We collect personal data in the following ways:

Data you provide directly

  • Contact form submissions: name, email address, company name, job title, and any information you include in your message.
  • HubSpot form submissions: the same fields above, processed via HubSpot (see Section 6).
  • Email correspondence: content of emails and messages you send us.

Data collected automatically

  • Usage data: pages visited, time on page, referring URL, browser type, operating system, device type.
  • IP address (anonymised where possible).
  • Cookies and similar technologies (see Section 5).

Data we do not collect

We do not collect special category data (health, religion, ethnicity, etc.) through this website. We do not collect payment information. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16.

03 Legal Basis for Processing

Under the GDPR (Regulation EU 2016/679), we rely on the following legal bases:

Processing Activity Legal Basis
Responding to contact form inquiries Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)) – responding to business inquiries
Sending follow-up communications after a form submission Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) – where indicated in the form
Analytics and website performance Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)) – improving our services
Essential cookies (site functionality) Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f))
Analytics and marketing cookies Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) – collected via cookie banner
Compliance with legal obligations Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c))
04 How We Use Your Data

We use the personal data we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide information about our services.
  • Contact you regarding a potential partnership or talent placement, where you have consented or we have a legitimate interest.
  • Manage our CRM and client relationships via HubSpot.
  • Analyse website traffic and improve user experience.
  • Comply with our legal and regulatory obligations.
  • Detect and prevent fraud or misuse of our website.

We do not use your data for automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects.

05 Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies. A cookie is a small text file stored on your device. You can manage your cookie preferences at any time via the cookie banner or your browser settings.

Category Purpose Consent Required
Essential Site functionality, session management, security. Cannot be disabled. No
Analytics Understand how visitors use the site (e.g. Google Analytics). Data is anonymised where possible. Yes
Marketing Track interactions for advertising and remarketing purposes (e.g. HubSpot tracking, LinkedIn Insight Tag). Yes

You may withdraw your consent at any time by clicking "Manage Cookies" in the footer, or by clearing cookies from your browser. Withdrawing consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.

06 Data Sharing and Transfers

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only where necessary:

  • HubSpot Inc. – CRM and form processing. HubSpot is certified under the EU-US Data Privacy Framework. Data may be stored on servers in the United States or EU.
  • Google Analytics – website analytics. We use IP anonymisation. Google LLC is certified under the EU-US Data Privacy Framework.
  • Hosting and infrastructure providers – our website is hosted on GitHub Pages (GitHub Inc., a Microsoft company), subject to their privacy policy.
  • Legal authorities – where required by law, regulation, or court order.

International transfers

Where data is transferred outside the European Economic Area (EEA), we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission, or transfers to countries covered by an adequacy decision.

07 Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purpose for which it was collected:

Data Type Retention Period
Contact form submissions 3 years from last interaction, or until deletion is requested
CRM records (HubSpot) 5 years from last interaction
Analytics data 26 months (Google Analytics default)
Cookie consent records 1 year from consent
Legal correspondence 10 years (legal obligation)

After the retention period expires, data is securely deleted or anonymised.

08 Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

👁️
Access
Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
✏️
Rectification
Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
🗑️
Erasure
Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
⏸️
Restriction
Ask us to limit how we process your data in certain circumstances.
📥
Portability
Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
🛑
Object
Object to processing based on legitimate interest or for direct marketing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@01talent.com. We will respond within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. In Portugal (our establishment): CNPD (www.cnpd.pt). In France: CNIL.

09 Children's Privacy

Our website and services are directed at businesses and professional adults. We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under the age of 16. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately at privacy@01talent.com and we will delete it promptly.
